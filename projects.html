Recruit Up!

A schedule generator for coaches in their recruiting process.

  • We generate a calendars for you based on your recruits. You then select which calendar works best for you. You are then able to schedule personal days within your calendar.
  • The head coach is able to assign events on the calendar to other coaches on the team.
  • You are able to see their current rating, the team the are on, and any notes that you may have taken about them
